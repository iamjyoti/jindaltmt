About Us.


Balajee Loha Limited (TMT Division).

Balajee Loha Limited (TMT Division) is an integral part of Balajee Group which has grown by leaps and bounds over the past 25 years to emerge as one of the largest groups in central India.

It is the new Automatic Steel Rolling unit of BALAJEE GROUP. The domain of the company covers the wide range of Corrosion Resistant Bars, Thermo Mechanically Treated Bars by adopting the most modern and advanced technology with current market requirement.

The story of their hard work and success started in the year 1988. The well managed organization is aggressively expanding and is constantly adding value in the area of Iron & Steel manufacturing and supply with the vision of taking the nation to the next level in all types of Structural Steel (Beam, Channel, Angle, Flat, etc.), Flat Steel (M.S. Plate, H.R. Sheet, C.R. Sheet, G.C. Sheet, Coil, etc) and Reinforcement Steel (TMT Bars, Wire Rods, H.B. WIRE, M.S. Pipe, etc) manufactured by SAIL, TATA, JINDAL, Lonneds, ESSAR, etc.

Mission and Responsibility

CONCRETE TMT firmly believes in giving back to the society. Since the community has so much faith in us and has given us a lot of recognition in the society, patronage and clientele to spread our business to the next level. therefore we at CONCRETE TMT are responsibly committed towards the society as a whole.

The systematic application of latest techniques and with the use of latest machinery, provides high quality end products and the adoption of new changes, upgradation and implementation of new technologies ensure proper and better quality products, the standard practice of BALAJEE GROUP.

We take special care to ensure that steps are taken to avoid industrial air pollution. We have introduced innovative technology, which does not require coal any more, which is one of the major contributors in air pollution. This unique technology includes TMT manufacturing process with the help of inducing advance production management technologies i.e. hot charging or hot direct charging technology, known as CCM (Continuous Casting Machine) casting. The basic idea behind this technology is giving quality product in a green and healthy way. We make sure that our thoughts are converted into actions to maintain the environmental sustainability for the generations to come.
